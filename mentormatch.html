PROBLEM

Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises play a critical role in the Indonesian economy. While their significance is undeniable, a considerable number of MSME entrepreneurs encounter difficulties in seeking guidance from experts or fellow entrepreneurs within the community. The abundance of MSMEs in Indonesia necessitates adequate training and easy access to guidance from industry experts and fellow entrepreneurs.

PROJECT OVERVIEW

Mentor Match a specially designed application to assist MSMEs in finding and engaging with experienced business mentors and fellow MSME entrepreneurs. By facilitating long-term relationships between mentors and mentees, Mentor Match aims to catalyze the growth and development of MSMEs in Indonesia.
