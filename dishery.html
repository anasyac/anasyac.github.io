PROJECT OVERVIEW

During the COVID-19 pandemic, numerous customers encounter challenges when attempting to secure reservations at multiple restaurants. The pandemic introduced unprecedented challenges in the restaurant reservation process, making it difficult for customers who seek to dine out at different establishments.

TEAM

Dishery is a revolutionary platform that simplifies the reservation process for customers during these uncertain times, providing them a seamless and stress-free dining experience. With Dishery, customers can effortlessly browse restaurant availability and secure reservations with ease.
