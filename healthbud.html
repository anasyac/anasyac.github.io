PROBLEM

Often, it becomes challenging for individuals to access vital medical information such as doctors' schedules, nearby hospitals, and their own medical records. The challenge encompasses a broad spectrum, ranging from obtaining doctors' schedules and locating nearby hospitals to accessing and managing personal medical records. The need for a streamlined and user-friendly solution becomes evident in addressing these challenges to simplify access to healthcare, which is a basic need of every individual.

PROJECT OVERVIEW

For the mid-term and final assignment of the course Platform-Based Programming (CSGE), my teammates () and I created HealthBud, a platform that provides a wealth of medical information at your fingertips. As an all-in-one healthcare companion, HealthBud aims to simplify access to healthcare information, enabling users to make informed decisions about their well-being anytime and anywhere.
